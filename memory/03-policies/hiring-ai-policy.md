# Hiring AI Policy

> Rules I follow when deciding whether to use AI for hiring-related tasks.

## What AI Can Do

- Draft job descriptions (with my review)
- Summarize long resumes into key bullet points
- Generate initial interview question banks
- Help me write rejection emails that are kind but clear

## What AI Cannot Do

- Make final hiring decisions
- Score or rank candidates without human review
- Access any candidate's personal data beyond what they've shared
- Generate reference check summaries without my approval

## Bias Prevention

- Always review AI-generated job descriptions for gendered language
- Don't use AI to filter by non-job-relevant criteria (location, name, gaps in employment)
- Treat AI output as a first draft, not a verdict

## Privacy Rules

- No candidate data stored in AI chat history
- No screenshots of resumes uploaded to third-party AI tools
- Candidate names anonymized when asking AI for assessment help

## Review Cadence

This policy is reviewed every 6 months or whenever a new AI tool is introduced to the hiring workflow.

# Start Here

Use this vault to stop repeating the same important information to AI.

## Step 1 — Read these

1. `WHY_MARKDOWN.md` – what a `.md` file is and why we use it.
2. `HOW_TO_SAVE_FROM_AI.md` – how to turn chats into reusable notes.
3. `HOW_TO_USE_WITH_OTHER_TOOLS.md` – how to move your notes into other apps.

## Step 2 — Fill in your basics

Start with three files:

- `memory/01-identity/about-me.md`  
- `memory/01-identity/preferences.md`  
- `memory/02-projects/current-projects.md`  

You can write in plain language. The goal is just to give AI a clean, reusable snapshot of who you are and what you’re working on.

## Step 3 — Save one real AI conversation

Pick a useful chat you had with an AI (about your work, your company, or your style).  
Follow the steps in `HOW_TO_SAVE_FROM_AI.md` to turn it into a single Markdown file in the right folder.

## Step 4 — Keep it small

Only save things you expect to reuse:

- how you like information presented  
- stable facts about you or your company  
- project context that will matter again  
- reusable prompts or instructions
